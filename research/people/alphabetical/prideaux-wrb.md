# Prideaux, W.R.B.

W. R. B. Prideaux (1880-1932)

Librarian. in post July 1921 - end of 1925

Arrived in the Library at Stratford Mews in July 1921, from the Reform Club, and worked part-time initially.

References: Symons 1993, p16
